Who is Behind All This?

My experiences includes covering events, taking portraits, and photojournalism. The greatest thing in the world, to me, is immersing myself in my work and not just drop-in and fly-out, but rather experience the moment for myself and learn about the people in front of the lens. While I’m finishing my broadcast degree at the University of North Texas, I was lucky to join a student-run photo agency at UNT where I started pursuing professional photography, and it was here where I served as the managing editor and business manager during the 2017-2018 school year.

A mentor once told me, “If you shoot strangers you get strange pictures.” This inspires to make a perfect image; though it will never be perfect, I will alway strive to achieve a perfect photo. Also, this quote enhances my curiosity with people, human interaction, and your experience in this relationship. Overall, my goal is to give you a priceless item that you can cherish forever and an experience worth bragging. Thanks for the love!
